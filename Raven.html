Raven's Skills List

Evolution 1: Advanced Tactics: Ranged weapon damage increased by 10%. Health increased by 10%.

Level 1: Lefty and Righty: Equipts two pistols that fire 6 times per second.
Level 2: Debilitating Shots: Dealing ranged damage applies one stack of Vulnerability, increasing damage taken from all sources by 5% on the target for 15 seconds. Stacks up to 3 times.
Level 5: Dual Debilitation: Lefty and righty now applies Debilitation Shots.
Level 8: Lingering Pain: The Vulnerability effect from Debilitating Shots can now stack up to 5 times.
Evolution 2: War Cry: Give a War Cry, affecting friendly characters within 4 tiles for 10 seconds. Grants 40% more damage and 40% attack speed for ranged weapons and 40% more damage and 16% attack speed for melee weapons.
Level 12: Practiced in Combat: Increases the duration of War Cry by 5 seconds.
Level 15: Explosive Rounds: Killing 10 enemies with a ranged weapon deals damage in a 1 tile radius.
Level 18: Where's Lefty: Elimination enemies with pistols reduces the cooldown of lefty and righty by one second.
Evolution 3: Frag Grenade: The Soldier throws a frag grenade detonating after a few seconds, dealing a base of 101 energy damage in a 0.5 tile radius.
Level 25: Bang and Pow: Dealing damage with Lefty and Righty causes an explosion that deals damage in a .5 tile radius.
Level 30: Kneecapper: Increases the effectiveness of Debilitating Shots Vulnerability stacks to 9%.
