Skirmisher's Skills List

Level 1: Throwing Stars: The Ninja hurls throwing stars in a straight line. Throws 1 star three times in a quick series, dealing a base of 35 edged weapon damage with each star.

Level 2: Mantis Leap: Ninja can leap once more while already jumping. Can only be executed once per jump.
Level 5: Trained Throw: Reduces the cost of Throwing Stars to 15 energy.
Level 8: Cascade: The Ninja throws one additional throwing star on the third toss. Throw pattern: 1/1/2.
Evolution 2: Dragon Slash: Ninja springs forward 2 tiles, dealing a base of 95 edged weapon damage to all enemies within 0.5
Level 12: Wings of the Dragon: Increases the length of the Dragon Slash to 3 tiles.
Level 15: Rain of Death: The Ninja throws one additional throwing star on the second toss. Throw pattern: 1/2/2.
Level 18: Return of the Dragon: Reduces the cost of Dragon Slash to 35 energy.
Evolution 3: Crescent Kick: A quick kick that does a base of 54 damage, 800.0 impact, and stuns for 3 seconds.
Level 25: Piercing Stars: Throwing Stars pierce through up to 3 targets.
Level 30: Tail of the Dragon: Dragon Slash leaves a persistent trail of energy, damaging for 10.5% of Dragon Slash damage, every 0.5 seconds, and slowing enemies by 30% caught within the tail for 3 seconds.
Description

Deadly mid-range fighter who combines Throwing Stars and Dragon Slash to create a storm of steel.
